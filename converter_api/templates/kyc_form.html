What is KYC?

KYC (Know Your Customer) is a process of verifying the identity of clients to ensure they are who they claim to be. It is a crucial step in preventing fraud, money laundering, and other financial crimes. By submitting your KYC documents, you help us maintain a secure and trustworthy environment for all users.
